A Legacy of Excellence

For over a century, AURUM has been at the forefront of haute joaillerie, creating pieces that transcend the ordinary and embrace the extraordinary. Founded in the heart of Geneva, our maison has built its reputation on an unwavering commitment to perfection.

Every creation that bears our name is a testament to the skill of our master artisans, who combine time-honored techniques with innovative design to craft jewelry of unparalleled beauty. From the initial sketch to the final polish, each piece undergoes a meticulous journey of creation that can take hundreds of hours.

Exceptional Materials

We source only the world's finest gems and precious metals, ensuring each piece meets our exacting standards of quality and ethical sourcing.

Master Craftsmanship

Timeless Design

We create pieces that transcend trends, designing jewelry that will be treasured for generations to come.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and policies.

We use only the finest materials in our creations. Our gold is available in 18K and 14K variations (yellow, white, and rose), while our platinum pieces are crafted from 950 platinum. All diamonds are conflict-free and certified by the Gemological Institute of America (GIA), and we source our gemstones from reputable mines with ethical practices.

Yes, we offer bespoke jewelry design services for clients seeking something truly unique. Our master artisans work closely with you to bring your vision to life, from initial concept sketches to the finished piece. Please contact our concierge team to schedule a private consultation.

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items in their original packaging. Custom-made pieces are final sale. To initiate a return, please contact our client services team who will guide you through the process and arrange for complimentary insured shipping.

All AURUM pieces come with complimentary professional cleaning and inspection services. We recommend bringing your jewelry to our boutique once a year for maintenance. Our artisans will carefully clean, polish, and check all settings to ensure your pieces remain in pristine condition.

Ethical sourcing is fundamental to our values. All our diamonds comply with the Kimberley Process and we work exclusively with suppliers who adhere to strict environmental and labor standards. Our gold is sourced from certified responsible mines, and we are committed to full transparency in our supply chain.

We offer complimentary ring sizing with every purchase. If you're unsure of your size, you can order our free ring sizer tool or visit any of our bouteries for a professional measurement. For surprise gifts, we recommend borrowing a ring they wear on that finger or consulting with our client advisors for guidance.

Every AURUM piece comes with a lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects. This covers repair or replacement of any piece that shows signs of faulty craftsmanship. Normal wear and tear, loss, or damage from accidents is not covered but can often be repaired for a nominal fee.
