Our Story

The Beginning

Born of a Goldsmith's Dream

In 1937, Jacques Auréa opened a small workshop on Rue de Rive in Geneva, armed with nothing but exceptional skill, an obsessive eye for detail, and an unshakeable belief that fine jewelry should be accessible to those who truly appreciate beauty.

Three generations later, his granddaughter Élise leads the atelier with the same guiding principle: every piece must be worthy of becoming an heirloom.

The Craft

Where Time Stands Still

Our Geneva atelier operates at a pace dictated entirely by craft, not commerce. We never rush. A single piece may pass through twelve pairs of hands before it is deemed ready — each artisan adding their signature touch of precision.

Stone setters, polishers, engravers — each a master of their discipline, each with decades of experience. This is not manufacturing. This is devotion.
