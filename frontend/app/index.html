Frequently Asked Questions
How many websites can I build for free?›
Free plan includes 5 generations per month and 1 deployed site. Upgrade anytime for more.
Which AI models can I use?›
Free plan includes basic models. Paid plans unlock all models including Claude Opus 4.6 and Gemini 3.1 Pro.
Can I use my own domain?›
Custom domains are available on the Pro plan. Free and Starter plans deploy to *.kenzoagent.com subdomains.
Can I download my code?›
Yes! All plans can copy code. Starter and above can download full project files.
How do I delete my account?›
Go to Account Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.
What is Brainstorm mode?›
Brainstorm mode lets you plan your website with AI before building. The AI asks questions and creates a strategy, then you hit "Build it" to generate the site.
