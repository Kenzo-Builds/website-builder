Frequently Asked Questions

How does NeuralOps integrate with my existing AI infrastructure? ▼
NeuralOps integrates seamlessly with any AI platform. Simply add our lightweight SDK to your codebase (Python, JavaScript, Go supported) or use our API. We also provide pre-built integrations for LangChain, OpenAI, Anthropic, and major cloud providers. Setup takes less than 5 minutes.
What's the pricing model? Can I scale down if needed? ▼
We offer flexible monthly billing based on API calls. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime without penalties. No long-term contracts. The Starter plan is perfect for development and testing, while Growth scales to millions of calls monthly.
Is my AI data secure and private on NeuralOps? ▼
Security is our top priority. We use end-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest. All infrastructure runs in ISO 27001 certified data centers. We never store your model inputs or outputs—only monitoring metadata. Enterprise customers can deploy on-premise for complete control.
Can I set up custom alerts and notifications? ▼
Yes. Growth and Enterprise plans include fully customizable alerts. Set thresholds for latency, error rate, cost, uptime, and more. Route alerts to Slack, PagerDuty, email, or webhooks. Create team-specific alert rules with our advanced filtering and ML-powered anomaly detection.
Do you offer a free trial? What's included? ▼
Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. You get full access to Starter features and $100 credit toward API usage. No credit card required. Most teams use the free trial to confirm integration works and validate the monitoring value before committing to a paid plan.
