Our Story

A legacy of artistry, passion, and an unwavering commitment to beauty.

The Beginning

Born from a Vision

Aurélia was founded in 1987 by Isabelle Aurélia, a third-generation jeweler who dreamed of creating pieces that transcended trends. Growing up in her grandmother's atelier in the Place Vendôme, she learned that true luxury is not about excess — it is about perfection in simplicity.

With a small team of master artisans, she opened the first Aurélia boutique on Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré, where the brand quickly became a whispered secret among Parisian connoisseurs of fine jewelry.

Craftsmanship

The Hands Behind the Beauty

Every piece of Aurélia jewelry passes through the hands of at least twelve artisans. Our gemologists travel to Antwerp, Mumbai, and Bogotá to personally select each stone, ensuring it meets our exacting standards for brilliance and ethical sourcing.

In our Parisian workshop, master goldsmiths employ techniques passed down through centuries — hand-setting stones, engraving intricate patterns, and polishing each surface to a mirror finish.

The result is jewelry that doesn't merely adorn — it tells a story of human dedication, patience, and the pursuit of the extraordinary.
