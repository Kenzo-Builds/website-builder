Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — for authentication and account recovery
  • Name — for display in your profile
  • Projects and generated content — stored to enable saving, editing, and deploying your websites

We may also collect optional profile information you provide: username, phone number, bio, location, and website.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the AI Web Builder service
  • To authenticate your account and protect against unauthorized access
  • To save and serve your projects and deployed websites
  • To improve the service based on usage patterns (anonymized)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • OpenAI / Anthropic / Google — AI model providers for website generation
  • Cloudflare — CDN and DNS

Your prompts are sent to AI providers to generate website content. We do not store AI provider API logs.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with row-level security policies. All connections use HTTPS encryption. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data through your profile page
  • Update your information at any time
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Export your projects via the download feature

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — for authentication sessions and user preferences (like theme settings). No tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

7. Deployed Websites

Websites you deploy through our service are publicly accessible at the subdomain you choose. You are responsible for the content of your deployed websites.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@kenzoagent.com
